/ 01 — Protocol

How Veritas Works

Step 01 🔐
Connect Wallet

Connect your Aptos wallet (Petra, Martian, or any AIP-62 compatible wallet). Your wallet address becomes your permanent, verifiable identity on Veritas.

Via: Aptos Wallet Adapter · AIP-62 Standard
Step 02 📝
Build Your Resume

Fill in your work experience, education, and skills. Your data is structured as a verifiable JSON object, ready for on-chain storage with cryptographic integrity.

Format: JSON Schema · Structured Data
Step 03 ⛓️
Publish On-Chain

Your resume JSON is uploaded to Shelby's decentralized hot storage. A cryptographic commitment is written to the Aptos blockchain — immutably linking you to your truth.

Via: Shelby SDK · @shelby-protocol/sdk · Aptos Mainnet
Step 04 🔗
Get Your Blob ID

Receive a unique Shelby Blob ID — a permanent, shareable identifier for your resume. Share this ID with employers, collaborators, or anyone who needs to verify your credentials.

Output: Shelby Blob Hash · Aptos Tx Hash
Step 05 ✅
Anyone Can Verify

Employers or collaborators enter your Blob ID into the Verify tab. The data is retrieved from Shelby and cross-checked against the Aptos blockchain commitment — trustlessly.

Via: Shelby RPC · Aptos Indexer
Step 06 ♾️
Permanent & Yours

No central authority can alter or delete your verified credentials. Your professional truth lives on a decentralized network — censorship-resistant and permanently accessible.

Network: Shelby Devnet → Mainnet · Aptos L1
